Limited Edition, Set of Two

What if the figure, who must be a woman, is alive within the object instead of just implied? The functionality is coupled with aesthetic pleasure in the marketplace, but here, the objects are independent. They are already performing a task as they are alive and indicate that they don’t need to be consumed in order to function, regardless if the said function is useful or needed.
