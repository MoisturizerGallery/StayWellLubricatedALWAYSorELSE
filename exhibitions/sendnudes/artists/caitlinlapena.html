In the Mirrored Room

I am an emerging visual artist who creates dreamlike drawings and paintings that capture details of strange domestic moments. My images are typically set within an interior space, recalling a game board where discordant objects and human appendages act as players surrounded by elements of nature and ideas of feminine refinement.

Recent exhibitions and publications include Night Drawings, Weird Things, Toronto, ON (2020); Free Spirit News, Issue 13, Portland, OR (2020); GIFC, The Hole, New York City, NY (2019); The Drawing Exchange Archive, Portland, OR (2019); Thresholds, Hamilton Artists’ Inc., Hamilton, ON (2016). I received a BFA in 2014 from Queen's University and currently live and work in Toronto, ON.
