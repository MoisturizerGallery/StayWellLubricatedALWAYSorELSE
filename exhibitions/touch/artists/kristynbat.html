Kristyn Bat Lopez is a parent of two toddlers, community art educator and tattoo artist. She wrote zines throughout high school and college and, faced with the most time off ever since those simpler times, has returned to the practice. You can submit a page to the community quarantine zine she is putting together through her nonprofit organization, Wayfaring Painter.
