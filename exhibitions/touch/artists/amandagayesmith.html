Amanda-Gaye Smith

Everything Is Just About The Same, 2020 // poem

Originally from the Virginian slip of Appalachia, Amanda-Gaye is a visual artist, bartender, and poet living in the lush swamp of Gainesville Florida. Currently she is focusing on “Anatomy of a Guest”, a full length book of poems about substance abuse and the service industry. In addition to her written work, she is also conceptualizing an installation set for the performance of her poems
