Katie Evans

Katie graduated from Flagler College in the fall of 2015 with a BFA in Fine Art and a BA in Graphic Design. After school she continued working at a design studio in the area. When she's not making art she enjoys practicing yoga, exploring Florida's state parks, and thrifting for objects that will inspire her next drawing.
