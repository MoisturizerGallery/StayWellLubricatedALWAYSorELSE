Touch Artist

Selina Wagner

Collapse, 2020 // graphite on paper // 53" x 56"

Born in 1998, Selina Wagner is an artist working in Gainesville, FL, pursuing her BFA with a specialization in Drawing from the University of Florida. Selina’s known for autobiographical drawings in graphite and charcoal. Her work can be seen as an intimate exploration of a multiple and evolving identity.

“Selina has exquisitely mastered the genre of portrait on paper as much as on canvas, creating a body of autobiographical pieces that are dense and as genuine as they could possibly be.” - Macarena Deij Prado.
