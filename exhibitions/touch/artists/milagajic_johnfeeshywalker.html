All My Marbles Are Lost, 2019 // video // 10:01

The collaborative practice between Mila Gajić and John 'Feeshy' Walker centers around narrative world building. Their 8 month long project series, Broaden Your Sense of Normal, reflects on the Gen Z experience of growing up amidst the school shooting epidemic. The pair intertwine experience, fiction, game culture, internet culture, and post-irony to explores themes of denialism, hopelessness, desensitization, social isolation, inactivity, and persecutory delusion.

Mila Gajić is a 3D artist and designer who creates immersive virtual spaces. Her work is inspired by her personal experiences, surroundings, and travels - she just makes stuff. She will graduate with a BFA in Art+Technology from the University of Florida in the fall of 2020.

John 'Feeshy' Walker is a multimedia artist whose work centers around developing lores. He utilizes video, 3D animation, VR, installation, performance, and sculpture to manifest portraits of these worlds which are larger reflections of contemporary issues, societal behaviors, and individual experiences. He graduated with a BFA in Art+Technology from the University of Florida in the spring of 2020.
