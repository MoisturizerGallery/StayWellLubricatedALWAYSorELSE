Touch Artist

Rebecca Matson

Tuesday Night, 2019 // oil on canvas // 58" x 28"

Rebecca Matson is an artist based in Gainesville, Florida, who graduated in 2020 with her BFA in Painting and a minor in Art History. She primarily creates large scale oil paintings based around themes of solitude, contemplation, the mundane, and enjoys working with color and space to create emotionally charged environments.
