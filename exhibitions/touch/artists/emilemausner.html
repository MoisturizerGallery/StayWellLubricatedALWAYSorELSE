Touch Artist

Emile Mausner

The Story, 2016 // acrylic on canvas // 24" x 24"

Emile Mausner is an Orlando-based artist and writer. In 2019, Mausner received her MFA with honors from the Yale School of Art. She holds bachelors degrees from New College of Florida in Sarasota and the University of Central Florida, where she studied Visual and Critical Studies and Studio Art, respectively.
